Quality Curve
=============


This mod makes higher Quality levels harder to obtain by adding extra (non-beneficial) Qualities in between Uncommon/Rare/Epic/Legendary. It also slightly increases the Chance to "jump" Quality Levels to compensate for the extra steps.

Inspired by a [Forum Post](https://forums.factorio.com/viewtopic.php?t=131249)
Forked from [Quality Plus](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/QualityPlus) by [orzelek](https://mods.factorio.com/user/orzelek)
